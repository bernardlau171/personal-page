Hi, I am Lau Ho Yeung Bernard from Hong Kong and welcome to my personal home page! I am a year 3 student at the Hong Kong Polytecnic university majoring in information security.

I did hit a few bumps along the way though. In my secondary school studies, I isn't keen to studying and my school results are pretty much rock bottom. I have to repeat two times, both in form 4 and form 5, but I decided to transfer to another school before my second repeat to finish my form 6 studies and go straight ahead to DSE. My DSE results aren't exactly good enough to have an offer from universities, so I change course to study for an associate degree in IT at HKCC.

After graduating from the associate degree of information technology in the Hong Kong Community College, I decided to continue developing my enthusiasm on IT and moved on to further my studies at PolyU. And so here I am!
